Resources
A resource is a statement of configuration policy.
It describes the desired state of an element of your infrastructure and the steps needed to bring that item to the desired state.
Ruby
Recipes are authored with Ruby syntax, and have an .rb file extension. They contain Chef resources.
However, Chef resources are not pure Ruby code.
First: Learn Resources
Learning Chef resources is far more important than understanding Ruby.
Learn about the most common resources, and their properties and actions before trying to learn Ruby.
