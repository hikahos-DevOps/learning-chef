Wrapper Cookbooks
• Don't use forked community cookbooks in production, or you will miss out on upstream changes
• Instead, use wrapper cookbooks to wrap upstream cookbooks and change their behavior without forking.
Wrapper Cookbook
• A wrapper cookbook encapsulates the functionality of the original cookbook.
• It defines new default values for the recipes.
• Chef community cookbooks can be used out of the box, but in most cases you will want to use them as a reference, as you write your own.
